The Bridge Between Projects and People

Our Mission

To build bridges between people, projects, and possibilities—through transparency, authenticity, and open dialogue that benefits everyone.

Clearing a Path to Yes.

Founded by Vance Pitzer, GreenLight Strategies is built on more than three decades of experience in real estate, real-estate finance, and strategic communication. We've advised developers, data-center operators, and civic organizations from Virginia to Atlanta to Seattle—navigating complex approvals, engaging communities authentically, and turning shared values into shared victories. Real progress happens when people talk to each other, not past each other. Every engagement is grounded in transparency, honesty, and the conviction that relationships—not transactions—create lasting results.

Goodworks Note

That same philosophy inspired GreenLight Goodworks, which applies our engagement strategies—at pro bono or low cost—to help nonprofits, churches, and community groups advance their causes and strengthen the communities we share.

How We Work — Five Principles

  • Transparency — Honest, open communication at every step.
  • Authenticity — Genuine engagement that earns trust, not spin.
  • Connection — Listening deeply and finding shared purpose.
  • Collaboration — Bringing stakeholders together for mutual success.
  • Intzgrity — Doing the right thing, even when it's not the easy thing.
