Helping Good People Do Good Work

GreenLight Goodworks is a community engagement initiative by GreenLight Strategies. We believe progress should serve people—not the other way around. Goodworks offers pro bono and low-cost support to nonprofits, churches, and community organizations, helping them engage stakeholders, tell their story, and clear a path to “yes.” We bring the same tools we use for large-scale development—listening sessions, outreach strategy, message framing, and coalition building—to the groups that make our towns stronger.
